About us

We are a full stack software engineering agency based out of London UK with over 14 years experience working with small startups and larger enterprises.

We are available for short to medium term contracting work, either on site in London or remotely around the world.

Our key skills are in Typescript (Angular and Node.js), HTML, CSS, Ionic, nx, webpack and Firebase.

  • We are extremely proficient at building mobile apps with the ionic framework, and have shipped full blown apps from concept to production in just a few months.
